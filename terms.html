Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made in writing. We accept cash, checks, and major credit cards. For recurring service plans, payment may be scheduled on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis as agreed.

2.2 Late Payment

Accounts not paid within 30 days may be subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month (18% annual percentage rate). We reserve the right to suspend services for accounts more than 30 days past due.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services. Our cancellation and refund policies are designed to be fair to both parties.

3.1 Appointment Cancellations

3.2 Service Plan Cancellations

Monthly service plans may be cancelled with 30 days' written notice. Annual plans may be subject to early termination fees as outlined in your service agreement. Refunds for prepaid services will be prorated based on services already rendered.

3.3 Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with our service, please contact us within 30 days. We will re-service your property at no additional charge. If the problem persists after re-treatment, we will refund your most recent service payment.

  • Notify us of any changes to property conditions that may affect treatment
  • Inform us of any health conditions, allergies, or sensitivities
